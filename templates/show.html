Description:-


-This circuit is quite useful where AC voltages fluctuate very much.
-It switches of the appliances when ac volt reaches more than 220 volt.
-In this circuit first ac is passed through transformer which lowers down its voltage then diode changes that ac in pulsating dc which is then -filtered for pure dc.
-When AC voltage is 220 Transistor is in cutoff region (OFF STATE).
-When voltage increases, negative voltage passes through zener diode and reaches to transistor base makes transistor in saturation region(ON STATE).
-Which makes relay in ON state and thus AC get tripped .
