5. 7905 IC 1
6. Electrolytic capacitor(22MF) 2

Description:-

Op-amp need negative voltage and we often wonder how we can get it.
This project will help you in making -5volt and +5 volt in one circuit.
Two types of voltage regulator IC is used here:-
(1) 7805 positive voltage regulator
(2) 7905 negative voltage regulator
The diode used here is 1n4007 .
AC on passing through the diode changes in pulsating DC.
The remaining pulses is then filtered by capacitor, output of capacitor then goes to regulator.
